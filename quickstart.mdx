---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Get Fittura installed and add your first wardrobe item in under 5 minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Fittura is built with Expo and React Native. You can run it in two ways:

    - **Expo Go (fastest)** — Install [Expo Go](https://expo.dev/go) on your iOS or Android device, then scan the QR code from a development server. No build step required.
    - **Native build** — Use EAS Build to compile a standalone `.apk` or `.ipa`. The app is identified by package `com.belalelkady.fittura` and slug `fittura`.

    <Note>
      Expo Go is recommended for development and testing. Native builds produced with EAS CLI are required for production distribution on the App Store or Google Play.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Open Fittura and tap **Sign up** on the welcome screen.

    Fill in your full name, email address, and a password. You can also choose your preferred language during registration — Fittura supports 10 languages.

    After submitting, Fittura sends a one-time passcode (OTP) to your email. Enter it on the verification screen to activate your account before you can sign in.

    Already have an account? Tap **Sign in** and enter your email and password. If your email is not yet verified, the app prompts you to re-send the OTP automatically.

    See [Authentication](/account/authentication) for details on password reset and session management.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first clothing item">
    After signing in, tap the **camera tab** at the bottom of the screen to open the **Add new items** screen.

    You have two options:

    - **Take a photo** — Tap the camera button. Fittura requests camera permission on first use, then lets you frame and capture a single item.
    - **Select from gallery** — Tap the gallery button to open your photo library. You can select multiple images in a single session.

    Once images are selected, tap **Save items** to upload them to your wardrobe. Each image is sent to the wardrobe service and stored against your account.

    <Tip>
      Select multiple items from your gallery at once instead of adding them one by one. The gallery picker supports multi-select, so you can upload an entire batch in a single save operation.
    </Tip>

    See [Camera & image picker](/features/camera) for full details on permissions, editing, and supported formats.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your first outfit recommendation">
    Go to the **home tab** and tap **Get Style**.

    Choose an occasion — for example, *Work*, *Casual*, *Date Night*, or *Gym* — or type a custom description. The AI analyses your wardrobe and returns up to five coordinated items along with style tips.

    Tap the heart icon on any recommendation to save it to your **Saved Outfits** tab for quick access later.

    See [AI recommendations](/features/ai-recommendations) for information on how occasion matching and item selection work.
  </Step>
</Steps>
