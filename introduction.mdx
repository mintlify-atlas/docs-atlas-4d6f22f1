## What Fittura does

Fittura connects your physical wardrobe to an AI styling engine. Here's how it works end to end:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your wardrobe">
    Take photos or upload images of your clothing. Fittura automatically classifies each piece by category (Top, Bottom, Shoes, etc.), season, and formality level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask for an outfit">
    Tell the AI what occasion you're dressing for — Date Night, Work, Gym, Casual, or any custom description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a recommendation">
    The AI selects up to five items from your wardrobe that work together, along with style tips and weather notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save what you love">
    Favorite the looks you want to revisit. Saved outfits appear in your **Saved Outfits** tab for easy access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
