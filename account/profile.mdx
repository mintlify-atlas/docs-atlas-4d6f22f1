## Account info

At the top of the **Profile** tab you can see your display name and email address. An avatar with your initials is generated automatically from your name — for example, "Alex Kim" becomes "AK".

## Editing your profile

Tap **Edit Profile** on the Profile tab. A bottom sheet modal slides up with your current name, email address, and a password field.

Make your changes and tap **Save Changes**. Only the fields you modify are updated — you can change one or all three in a single save.

### Changing your name

Update the **Full Name** field in the Edit Profile modal and tap **Save Changes**.

### Changing your email

Update the **Email Address** field and tap **Save Changes**. Fittura sends a confirmation email to your new address. The change takes effect after you confirm it from your inbox.

### Changing your password

Enter a new password in the **New Password** field and tap **Save Changes**. Leave the field blank if you do not want to change your password.

## Dark mode

The Profile tab includes a dark mode toggle with a sun or moon icon. Switching the toggle immediately updates the entire app's color theme. Your preference is saved and restored the next time you open Fittura.

<Tip>
  Dark mode is easier on the eyes in low-light environments and can help reduce battery usage on OLED screens.
</Tip>

## Signing out and account deletion

The **Sign Out** and **Delete Account** controls are also on the Profile tab. See [Authentication](/account/authentication) for details on those actions.
