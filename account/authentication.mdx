title: "Authentication"
description: "Create an account, sign in, reset your password, and manage your session in Fittura."
---

## Creating an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app and tap 'Sign Up'">
    On the login screen, tap **Sign Up** at the bottom to navigate to the registration screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Enter your full name, email address, and a password. Passwords must be at least 6 characters long.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Sign Up'">
    Tap the **Sign Up** button. You are automatically taken to the email verification screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your verification code">
    Check your inbox for an email from Fittura containing an OTP code. Enter the code in the verification screen and tap **Verify Email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Fittura">
    Once your email is verified, you are taken directly to the main wardrobe screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Email verification is required before you can access the app. If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, tap **Resend Code** on the verification screen.
</Note>

## Signing in

On the login screen, enter your registered email address and password, then tap **Sign In**. If your email has not been verified yet, you are prompted to complete verification before continuing.

## Resetting your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap 'Forgot Password'">
    On the login screen, tap the **Forgot Password** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type in the email address associated with your account and tap **Send Reset Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Look for an email from Fittura containing a reset code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type the code into the verification field on the next screen and tap **Verify Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Enter your new password and confirm it, then tap **Reset Password**. You are returned to the login screen once the reset is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing out

Go to the **Profile** tab and tap **Sign Out**. You are returned to the login screen and your local session is cleared.

## Deleting your account

Tap **Delete Account** at the bottom of the Profile tab. A confirmation dialog appears — tap **Delete** to confirm. Your account, all wardrobe items, and saved outfits are permanently removed.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All wardrobe items, outfit suggestions, and account data are immediately and irreversibly erased.
</Warning>
