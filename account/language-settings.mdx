---
title: "Language settings"
description: "Switch Fittura's interface to one of 10 supported languages, including Arabic with RTL support."
---

Fittura supports 10 languages. Your language preference is saved locally and restored automatically each time you open the app.

## Changing your language

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Profile tab">
    Tap **Profile** in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Language row">
    Find the **Language** row in the settings list. It shows the flag and name of your currently selected language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a language">
    A language picker sheet slides up displaying all available options. Your current language is highlighted with a checkmark.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap your preferred language">
    The interface updates immediately after you make a selection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported languages

| Language | Code | Script direction |
|---|---|---|
| English | en | LTR |
| Spanish (Español) | es | LTR |
| French (Français) | fr | LTR |
| German (Deutsch) | de | LTR |
| Italian (Italiano) | it | LTR |
| Portuguese (Português) | pt | LTR |
| Arabic (العربية) | ar | RTL |
| Japanese (日本語) | ja | LTR |
| Korean (한국어) | ko | LTR |
| Chinese (中文) | zh | LTR |

## Arabic and RTL support

Switching to Arabic automatically enables a right-to-left layout across the entire app — navigation, text alignment, and component order all flip to match Arabic reading direction.

<Note>
  Switching from or to Arabic may require restarting the app for all layout changes to take full effect.
</Note>

## What gets translated

All user-facing text is localized, including category names, tag labels, button text, and error messages. Content you add yourself — such as custom clothing item names — is stored as entered and is not translated automatically.
