---
title: "Saved outfits"
description: "Save your favorite AI-recommended looks and access them anytime."
---

The Saved Outfits tab stores every outfit you have hearted from AI recommendations. Saved outfits persist across sessions using local device storage. The number of saved outfits is shown in the subtitle beneath the screen title.

## Browsing saved outfits

Each saved outfit is displayed as a card containing:

- **Name** — the AI-generated title for the look.
- **Reasoning** — the AI's explanation of why the items work together.
- **Item strip** — a horizontally scrollable row of item photos, each labeled with its category beneath the image.

## Viewing item images

Tap any item photo in a saved outfit to open the fullscreen image viewer. The viewer shows the item's category in a footer overlay at the bottom of the screen.

## Removing saved outfits

Tap the trash icon on an outfit card to remove it from your saved outfits. This action only removes the saved look — it does not delete the individual clothing items from your wardrobe.

## Empty state

When no outfits have been saved yet, the screen shows a heart icon and a prompt to use the AI stylist.

<Tip>
