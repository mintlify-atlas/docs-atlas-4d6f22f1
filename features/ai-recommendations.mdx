---
title: "AI outfit recommendations"
description: "Get personalized outfit suggestions from your wardrobe for any occasion."
---

The AI stylist picks up to 5 items from your wardrobe that work together for a chosen occasion. It returns a named outfit with a reasoning summary, style tips, and weather notes.

<Note>
Recommendations require a non-empty wardrobe. Add items first via the [Camera tab](/features/camera) before requesting a suggestion.
</Note>

## Getting a recommendation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the AI stylist">
    Tap **Get Style** in the top-right corner of the Wardrobe tab. This button is disabled when your wardrobe is empty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an occasion">
    Select one of the preset occasions — Date Night, Work, Gym, or Casual Hangout — to generate a recommendation immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Or enter a custom occasion">
    Type any occasion into the text field and tap the sparkle button to submit it. The field accepts free-form text such as "beach holiday" or "wedding guest".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the AI">
    The app sends your wardrobe data to the n8n automation workflow. A loading indicator is shown while the AI analyzes your items and builds the outfit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported occasions

| Occasion | Description |
|---|---|
| Date Night | Evening looks with a romantic tone |
| Work | Professional and office-appropriate outfits |
| Gym | Athletic and activewear combinations |
| Casual Hangout | Relaxed everyday styling |
| Custom | Any free-text occasion you type in |

## Understanding your recommendation

The result screen shows:

- **Outfit name** — a short title generated by the AI for the look.
- **Reasoning** — a sentence explaining why these items work together for the occasion.
- **Item list** — up to 5 items, each displayed with its photo, category, and description. Tap any photo to open the fullscreen image viewer.
- **Style tips** — a bulleted panel with practical styling advice (shown when available).
- **Weather notes** — a note about how the outfit suits current or seasonal conditions (shown when available).

## Saving recommendations

Tap the heart icon in the outfit header to save the look to your Saved Outfits. The heart turns red to indicate the outfit has been saved. Tapping it again while it is already saved has no effect — each outfit is saved only once.

Saved outfits are available in the [Saved Outfits tab](/features/favorites).

## Trying again

Tap **Try Another** at the bottom of the result screen to return to the occasion picker and generate a new recommendation.

<Info>
The AI uses n8n automation workflows to process your wardrobe data and generate outfit suggestions. See [/reference/n8n-webhooks](/reference/n8n-webhooks) for configuration details.
</Info>
